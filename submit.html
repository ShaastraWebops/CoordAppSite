Coordinator App Submission

Submission Guidelines

  1. Recent grade card needs to be attached in the application. Workflow screenshots are also acceptable.
  2. Submit your apps only in PDF format.
  3. Name your application as Name_RollNum_Department_SubDepartment.pdf
  4. There are two steps in the submission process.
    • Mail your apps to the corresponding cores whose details can be found here.
    • Fill the form. Smail login is needed to fill the form.
